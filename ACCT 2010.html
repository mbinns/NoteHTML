Notes Payable

  • Notes payable looks at the same transaction from the view point of the borrower
  • Key events:
    • Establish the note
    • accruing interest incurred but not paid
    • recording interest paid
    • recording principle paid 
      On Nov 1st 2014 Company x recieved an 8000 six month 6% note to settle an 8000 unpaid balance owed by Zeus Corp

Establish the note
Nov 1 2014
	dr. Accounts payable 8000
	    cr. Note payable     8000

Accrue for interest incurred but not yet paid
Dec 31st 2014
    dr. Interest expense     80
	    cr. Interest payable   80
		
Record interest and principle payment
May 1st 2015
	dr. Interest expense 160
	dr. Interest payable 80
	dr. Note payable     8000
	   cr. Cash				 8240
