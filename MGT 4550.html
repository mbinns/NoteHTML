Technology Ecosystem

The Business Problem

  • The main challenge is the dynamic nature of the who rapidly changing IT environment
  • We need more formal frameworks and tools to help see future landscapes
  • Leveraging the power of new technology requires a holistic view of the technologies context

Managing Technology

  • Requires systems thinking:
    • A whole group of processes or objects that act as a unit of interacting part

Systems

  • Economic Systems:
    • How money is distributed:
      • Example) Facebook sells our information
  • Social Systems:
    • How it impact social interaction:
      • Example) Divorce has gone up since Facebook has become more prevalent
  • Ecological Systems:
    • How it impacts the world:
      • Example) Facebook has a high carbon footprint because they have to have high power consumption in their server farms
  • Every system has interrelated parts related to one another - Subsystems:
    • Changes in subsystems will often result in changes to other parts of the system
