Chapter 1 Managerial Account and Cost Concepts

  • What is Managerial Accounting:
    • Designed to provide info to people within the business
  • Segments:
    • Geographical area
    • Type of business
    • Different business segments:
      • Look to see how well each segment is doing
  • There are no standards that are mandatory in managerial accounting
  • Three vital activities:
    • Planning (Budgeting)
    • Controlling:
      • Feedback and Feedforward mechanisms
      • Performance reports:
        • What should have happened with the budgeted figures in a period of time
        • What did happen with the budgeted figures in a period of time
        • Sometimes an unfavorable variance is not a negative but it is still flagged on a PR
    • Decision making:
      • Selecting a course of action from competing alternatives
      • Making data driven decisions
  • Professional Certs:
    • Certified Management Accountant (CMA)
    • Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
    • Certified Internal Auditor (CIA)
